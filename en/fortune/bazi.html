What is BaZi? Understanding the Eight Characters

BaZi (八字), literally meaning "Eight Characters," is one of the most profound systems of Chinese metaphysics and astrology. Also known as the Four Pillars of Destiny (四柱推命), this ancient practice has been guiding people's life decisions for over a thousand years. Unlike Western astrology which maps planetary positions, BaZi creates a unique energetic blueprint based on your birth date and time using the Chinese calendar's Heavenly Stems (天干) and Earthly Branches (地支).

Your BaZi chart is composed of four pillars — Year, Month, Day, and Hour — each containing a Heavenly Stem and an Earthly Branch, forming eight characters in total. The Day Stem is particularly significant as it represents you, the "Day Master," and forms the center around which the entire chart is interpreted.
