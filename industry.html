🏭 Industries We Source

Hengshui is home to some of China's most competitive manufacturing clusters. From wire mesh to baijiu, medical devices to musical instruments — connect directly with factories.

Wire Mesh & Fencing
安平 · Anping

The world's largest wire mesh production hub. Anping produces 80% of China's wire mesh exports. 30+ years of manufacturing heritage.

  • Welded Wire Mesh Panels
  • Stainless Steel Wire Mesh
  • Chain Link Fencing
  • Hexagonal Wire Netting
  • Expanded Metal Mesh
  • Barbed Wire & Razor Wire
  • Window Screen Mesh

Baijiu & Chinese Spirits
衡水老白干 · Hengshui Laobaigan

Home of Hengshui Laobaigan, one of China's oldest baijiu brands with 1900+ years of history. Light aroma (清香型) style, 67° classic expression.

  • Hengshui Laobaigan Classic 67°
  • Laobaigan Aged Series (5/10/20 Year)
  • Gift Box & Ceremonial Sets
  • White Label / OEM Baijiu

Medical Devices
衡水医疗器械基地

One of China's largest medical device manufacturing bases. Specializing in rehabilitation equipment, orthopedic supports, and disposable medical supplies.

  • Rehabilitation & Mobility Aids
  • Orthopedic Braces & Supports
  • Hospital Beds & Furniture
  • Surgical Face Masks & PPE
  • Disposable Medical Supplies

Musical Instruments
衡水乐器制造

China's premier wind instrument manufacturing region. Western brass & woodwind instruments made with precision craftsmanship. OEM/ODM available.

  • Trumpets, Trombones, French Horns
  • Saxophones (Alto, Tenor, Soprano)
  • Clarinets, Flutes, Oboes
  • Marching Band Instruments
  • Student & Professional Lines

Timepieces & Clocks
衡水钟表制造

Traditional clock and watch manufacturing cluster. Mechanical and quartz movements, wall clocks, desk clocks, and custom OEM timepieces.

  • Mechanical Wall Clocks
  • Desk & Mantel Clocks
  • Custom Logo / Promotional Clocks
  • Quartz Clock Movements
  • Wooden & Metal Case Clocks

FRP & Composite Materials

Major fiberglass reinforced plastic (FRP) production base. GRP pipes, tanks, grating, cooling towers, and custom composite products for industrial applications.

  • FRP/GRP Pipes & Fittings
  • Molded FRP Grating
  • FRP Storage Tanks
  • Cooling Towers
  • Custom Composite Fabrication
