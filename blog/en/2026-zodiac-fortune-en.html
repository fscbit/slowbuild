Part 1: 2026 Horse Year Overview

2026 is the lunar year of Bing Wu (丙午), the Fire Horse. The Heavenly Stem is Bing Fire and the Earthly Branch is Wu Fire — a double Fire combo, making this a classic high-fire year. Bing Fire is solar fire: radiant, warm, and bold. Wu Fire is peak fire: bursting with vitality and energy. In a year this fiery, the key is keeping the Water-Fire balance in check.

Those born in the Year of the Horse face their zodiac year (Ben Ming Nian) in 2026. This means heightened volatility across the board and extra caution is warranted. Beyond the Horse, the Rat (clashing Tai Sui), Ox (harming Tai Sui), and Rabbit (breaking Tai Sui) also offend Tai Sui in 2026.

Geographically, the south and southwest are the auspicious directions in 2026 — these areas carry strong positive energy, ideal for your workspace or home. The Tai Sui direction is due south, so avoid renovation or digging in that zone.
