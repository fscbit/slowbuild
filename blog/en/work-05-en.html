Paycheck Gone in 3 Days? BaZi Wealth Analysis + Feng Shui Remedies to Stop the Money Drain

June 21, 2026 · 7 min read

It's the 15th—payday. You excitedly open your banking app. After deducting rent, credit cards, utilities, phone bill... balance: ¥473.58. You do the math—you need to survive until next month's 15th. You quietly remove items from your shopping cart.

Then over the next two weeks, absurd things happen: phone screen cracks (repair: ¥300), roommate suddenly announces they're moving out next month and you need to front double deposit (¥2,000), company team-building AA split that you forgot to budget (¥268), transit card somehow double-charged—

You stare at your bills and ask the heavens: "Which god of wealth did I offend?"

If your problem lately isn't "low salary" but "money won't stay"—your income isn't terrible, but some force keeps draining it—you may be experiencing what destiny analysis calls "Wealth Star under attack."

"Wealth" in BaZi: Not How Much You Earn—How Much You Keep

BaZi wealth analysis centers on two things: Direct Wealth and Indirect Wealth.

  • Direct Wealth is your monthly salary—income from labor. Stable Direct Wealth means at least you won't starve; steady monthly intake.
  • Indirect Wealth is income beyond salary—bonuses, side hustles, investment returns, even lottery wins. Strong Indirect Wealth means windfalls, but also easy windfall losses.

Common misconception: "The stronger the Wealth Star, the better." Wrong. When Wealth Stars are super strong but the Day Master is weak (your own energy insufficient), you hit an awkward situation: money arrives, but you can't hold onto it.

Like a large bowl on an unstable table—fill it with all the water you want, one shake and it all spills. In BaZi, this is called "Weak body cannot sustain wealth."

Are you the "weak body, abundant wealth" type?
□ Paycheck arrives, you spend like crazy for a few days, then scrape by until month-end
□ Windfalls (bonuses, refunds, red envelopes) always followed by unexpected expenses (repairs, fines, wedding gifts)
□ Lend money to friends and never chase it up—eventually forget yourself
□ No concept of money, never do the math—check balance at month-end to see where it all went
Hit 3 or more? You're likely the abundant-Wealth-weak-body type. Money is like sand—the harder you grip, the faster it leaks.

Why You're Losing Money: 4 Most Common BaZi Causes

1. Wealth Star Under Clash

The Wealth Star in your BaZi (representing money) is being assaulted by this year's Earthly Branch. Your money bag has a hole punched in it—incoming speed unchanged, outgoing speed accelerated. 2026 is Bing Wu year: Wu Fire clashes with Zi Water. If your Wealth Star is Water, you're in the crosshairs.

2. Peer (Bi Jie) Robbing Wealth

Peer represents your equals—colleagues, competitors. During Peer-heavy years: colleagues get raises, you don't. Friends borrow and don't repay. Roommate cheats you on rent. Team bonus gets carved up unfairly. Essentially: people on your level are taking your share.

3. Weak Output Can't Generate Wealth

Output represents your talent and creativity—the tool that generates wealth. You have money-making ideas but can't execute, or you execute but get no results. Output is too weak to convert ideas into income.

4. Too Much Wealth, Too Weak a Body

As above—your Day Master can't sustain abundant wealth. Classic symptom: any time you save money, something happens. Save a few thousand, immediately get sick and spend a few thousand on treatment. Get a year-end bonus, landlord announces a rent hike.

Feng Shui Remedies: Stop the Leak Without Spending a Fortune

1. Check Your Wealth Corner (Most Important)

Every home has a wealth corner—typically the diagonal opposite corner from the main door. Check yours:

  • Trash can there? → Remove immediately. Trash in the wealth corner = using a garbage bin as a piggy bank.
  • Cluttered? → Clear it. The tidier the wealth corner, the more money wants to stay.
  • Facing the bathroom door? → Add a curtain or screen. Bathroom is an "unclean" zone—direct line to wealth corner means flushing your money away.
  • Dark corner, no light? → Turn on a lamp. Wealth corners need to be bright.

2. Wallet / Phone Case Color

Choose wallet color based on your Useful God. Minimum-cost Feng Shui adjustment:

  • Favors Metal: White, silver wallet
  • Favors Wood: Green, teal wallet
  • Favors Water: Black, deep blue wallet
  • Favors Fire: Red, purple wallet
  • Favors Earth: Yellow, brown wallet

Even just changing your phone case color can tweak your "wealth color palette."

3. The Fridge & Stove Secret

In Feng Shui, the fridge and kitchen represent "food fortune"—if you have food on the table, wealth follows. Two simple adjustments:

  • Don't let the fridge go empty. Even just a carton of milk or a few eggs. An empty fridge is a "no-wealth sign" in Feng Shui.
  • Stove shouldn't face the faucet. Fire (stove) facing Water (faucet) is "Fire-Water clash"—in wealth terms, money comes and immediately gets flushed away. Add a partition or move one.

4. The Front Door: Your "Wealth Intake"

Your front door is where wealth energy enters. Three things:

  • No pile of shoes. A stinky shoe mountain at the door—the wealth god runs before even entering.
  • Must be bright. Higher wattage bulb.
  • Don't let the door directly face a window. This is "piercing hall energy"—money enters the front and exits the back. Add a screen or door curtain.

Targeted Remedies by Money-Leak Type

🎯 Specific Remedies for Each Type

  • Wealth Star under clash: Find your Wealth Star direction (chart will show). Place something red or gold there to "stabilize." E.g., Wealth Star in the east—put a small lamp there, always on.
  • Peer robbing wealth: Reduce partnerships, loans to friends, joint investments. Keep money in hard-to-access accounts (fixed deposits, separate savings card).
  • Weak Output: It's not your earning ability—it's your "conversion channel" that's blocked. Break your idea into the smallest possible step and do step one today—even if it's just registering an account.
  • Too much wealth, weak body: Strengthen "Resource Star" energy—Resource protects you. Read more, learn more, find someone who's got your back. Physical energy boost beats any financial hack.

Final Word

Destiny analysis helps you see why money leaks. But saving money ultimately comes down to self-discipline. The best Feng Shui can't outrun a daily boba tea habit plus nightly online shopping. I've seen people fill their homes with wealth-attracting trinkets, yet still can't pay off their credit cards—because they bought a houseful of wealth-attracting trinkets.

Destiny + Self-Discipline = Best Financial Plan. First figure out your money-leak type, then apply targeted remedies. As for saving—start today. Even ¥10 a day is better than nothing.
