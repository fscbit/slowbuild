Life Path Numbers 1-9: The Complete Guide to Your Birth Code

June 21, 2026 · Life Path Numbers

What Is a Life Path Number? How Do You Calculate It?

The Life Path Number is the most important number in numerology — it represents your life's theme and direction. The calculation is incredibly simple:

Add all the digits of your birth year, month, and day together. Keep reducing until you get a single digit (1-9).

Example: Born May 20, 1990 → 1+9+9+0+5+2+0 = 26 → 2+6 = 8. Life Path Number is 8.

Note: If your sum results in 11, 22, or 33, these are "Master Numbers" — don't reduce them further. They carry special meaning and higher spiritual purpose.
